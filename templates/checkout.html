OUR STORY

A short story about how and why Njumu was started.

The idea behind Njumu started sometime last year, we were invited to a rather official meeting and one of the requirements was we all had to show up looking sharp. At the moment, most of us didn't own a decent pair of official shoes.
So we set out to look for a pair or two. It was an uphill task, we had to go through a series of shops trting to bargain the overpriced offers we were getting. That's when we got the idea of founding NJUMU, a one stop platform to get affordable NEW & MTUMBA shoes without compromising on quality.
